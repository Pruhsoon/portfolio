## Social Return on Investment (SROI)

SROI is modeled by comparing the present value of social benefits ($PV$) to the total value of investment ($I$):

$$\text{SROI} = \frac{\sum_{t=1}^{n} \frac{B_t}{(1 + r)^t}}{I}$$

Where:
- $B_t$ is the quantified social benefit in year $t$.
- $r$ is the discount rate.
- $I$ is the initial capital investment.

An SROI ratio of $3:1$ implies that every $\$1$ invested generates $\$3$ of social value.

# NSUT Debating Society

The Netaji Subhas University of Technology (NSUT) Debating Society is one of the premier debating clubs in the national circuit, specializing in Parliamentary Debating (British Parliamentary and Asian Parliamentary formats).

## Format Specifications

Parliamentary debating requires rapid analysis, logical mapping, and structure. The core scoring function $S$ for a debate team can be modeled as a function of matter ($M$), manner ($m$), and method ($d$):

$$S = w_1 M + w_2 m + w_3 d$$

Where the weights satisfy $\sum w_i = 1.0$.

## Society Structure

1. **Executive Council (EC)**: Directs society training modules, handles public relations, and schedules registrations.
2. **Adjudication Core**: Trains novice members in tournament scoring systems, motion adjudication, and speaker tracking.
3. **General Body Members (GBMs)**: Active debaters and speakers participating in weekly practice debates.
